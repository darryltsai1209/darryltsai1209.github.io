Startup Competition Pitch Deck

The combination of the automatic line built by our team and the worker conveys our core competency. We aim to pass down traditional crafts by transforming them into digital assets for robots. The picture of mature automatic line also demonstrates our ability.

At the beginning, the pitch deck uses conspicuous text to show the potential of robotics metalworking market and the problems traditional factories facing now. This can help listeners to get into the pitch quickly.

Then I visualize the manufacturing process used by workers and robots respectively with simple icons, which can make listeners be familiar with our solution. In this way, the technique won’t be too tough for them to understand.

After the business model, our roadmap is visualized as a tree. The imagery of it is that our techniques will grow into branches – the different applications, and the nutrients will be transport back to the root – our database. Then a seedling – the service – will be raised and help us to enter the global market.

To convince listeners that we have ability to make profits, only the demonstration of our business plan is not enough. Apart from the proof of our performance, a successful story is attractive as well. The improvements are arranged like stairs that symbolize the growth, and the black and white picture of workers indicates the fading away generation.

I try to close the presentation with a powerful ending. The image of a faded senior craftsman embracing a robot represents our belief clearly – we make robots not only equipment, but also apprentices to preserve heritage from experienced craftsmen.
